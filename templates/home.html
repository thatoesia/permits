Department of Labour and Social Security
Overview

The Department of Labour and Social Security in Botswana is a government department responsible for formulating and implementing policies, programs, and regulations related to labor and social security matters in the country. Its primary role is to promote decent work, protect workers' rights, and ensure social security coverage for the population.

Functions and Responsibilities
  • Labor Relations: Facilitating and promoting harmonious industrial relations between employers and employees.
  • Labor Inspection: Conducting inspections and monitoring compliance with labor laws and regulations.
  • Employment Services: Providing job placement, career counseling, and skills development programs.
  • Social Security: Administering social security schemes and programs for social protection.
  • Labor Market Information: Collecting and analyzing labor market data for policy formulation and planning.
  • Occupational Health and Safety: Promoting and enforcing workplace health and safety regulations.
  • Employment Regulation for Foreign Nationals: Regulating the employment of foreign nationals in Botswana.

Department of Immigration and Citizenship
Overview

The Department of Immigration and Citizenship is a government department responsible for managing immigration and citizenship matters in the country. Its primary role is to control the entry and stay of foreign nationals, enforce immigration laws, and facilitate the acquisition of citizenship for eligible individuals.

Functions and Responsibilities
  • Visa Services: Processing and issuing visas for individuals wishing to enter the country for various purposes.
  • Immigration Control: Enforcing immigration laws and regulations to manage the entry and stay of foreign nationals.
  • Border Security: Ensuring the security of the country's borders and implementing measures to prevent illegal immigration.
  • Passport Services: Issuing passports to citizens for international travel and managing passport-related services.
  • Citizenship Acquisition: Facilitating the acquisition of citizenship for eligible individuals through naturalization or other legal means.
  • Immigration Policies: Formulating and implementing immigration policies and regulations in alignment with national interests.
  • International Cooperation: Collaborating with international partners and organizations on matters related to immigration and citizenship.
